Treasure
How to play
Shuffle a deck of 52. Deal 2 to each player. All players share the same deck (face down) & discard pile (face up).
Take turns until someone wins by collecting 3 Treasure (Ace) cards.
Whenever the deck runs empty, shuffle the discard pile to form a new deck.
On your turn
First, you may play one card from your hand. A card with † stays in play on your side; otherwise, it is discarded.
At the end of your turn discard any number of cards, holding no more than your limit (2); Then draw until you reach your limit.
